Digital satellite imagery and new sensor technology provides unprecedented opportunities to create new views and images of the Earth, the rocks and landscapes exposed on it's surface and of the natural processes in action that shape these over time. These images can be useful as a form of investigative scientific research into how the Earth works, as novel teaching materials, or they can simply bring joy to the viewer from an entirely aesthetic place.

At geopixel we create images for all of these reasons, and can provide custom images in virtually any format, at any resolution, and of pretty much anywhere on Earth.

Most of these we share with anybody, at no cost with no strings attached whatsoever. We are of course also also happy to discuss custom projects or special requests for a modest fee.
